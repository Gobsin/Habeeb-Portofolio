About

Preamble

Habeeb is a lawyer with experience in intellectual property, entertainment, and technology law, with a strong focus on advisory work for innovative and creative industries. His core practice centers on IP protection, commercialization, and regulatory strategy.

He is WIPO-certified, holding nine WIPO Academy certifications, and has published widely on IP law, including in WIPO Magazine. He has contributed to policy discussions in the space, including supporting WIPO Nigeria on IP-related recommendations and serving as a panelist at national IP conferences.

His work spans Asia, Africa, and the Middle East, advising startups, technology companies, and creative businesses on IP structuring, compliance, licensing, and digital regulatory frameworks — from national trademark portfolios to cross-border digital asset compliance.
